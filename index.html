Juan Pablo Lugo

Instructional Content Coach

Juan Pablo Lugo works at ESC Region 11 as an Early Childhood Coach with Texa. School Ready through the Children's Learning Institute. He also develops and delivers professional development for school district personnel in the Region 11 area. He has previously worked as an Elementary School Assistant Principal in Irving ISD and as a Dual Language and Bilingual Education Consultant for Region 10 Education Service Center. He has experience teaching early childhood and bilingual education. He holds a B.S. in Psychology from University of the Sacred Heart in Puerto Rico and a M.Ed. in Education Administration from University of North Texas in Denton.
